The game

White Panther is a god game developed by Oscar Pons and Oriol Sabate, students of CITM (Barcelona), due to the subject Artificial Intelligence at third course. The purpose of the game is to develop a coordinate system of behaviour trees that feel inclusive to the player as they respond as expected when interacting with them. Make the people happy to rise your reputation, selling pieces is not your main purpose, but you have to earn money to improve your Jeweller's. Give the best quality to your customers or your reputation will be punished.

Chaos can be your ally or your enemy, you decide

Intense gameplay

Short game duration but a high intensive experience. The shop will get out of the way if you can not handle it, is up to you to properly manage money and invest in what is needed, don't forget to save some earning for the daily restock.

group

Extremelly diverse

The game has more than 30 different types of characters, from a business man willing for a nice watch to a kind grandpa who wants some pearls to present his wife. Don't expect to see the same characters over and over.

settings

Shop upgradable

All business starts at floor. You lucky, start with a nice jewelry shop that can be upgraded with the simple action of a click, oh, and the money it costs!

Upgrade the shop to increase your chances

Information

Your flow of costumers depends on the fame you have, it can not be bought but improved with good managing and happy costumers.
Clients probability to buy something is determined by the fame you have and a random factor per client, also the behaviour of the clients is not much predictable as it involves randomness inside its hearth. Your main tasks will be balancing the shop keeper to serve the costumers as well as keep him working to restock the products of the shop, buying stock ath the end of the day and upgrade the shop.
Camera is controlled as expected with Left mouse button and WASD or SPACE. FPS like.
