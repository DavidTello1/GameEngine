Davos 1.0.0

Download
Direct download

Davos Engine is a 3D game engine developed by Oscar Pons and David Tello, students of CITM (Barcelona), due to the subject Game Engines at third course. It uses common libraries such as OpenGL 4.6.0, Glew 2.1.0, Simple DirectMedia Layer (SDL 2.0.10), ImGui 1.73 and DevIL 1.8.0
